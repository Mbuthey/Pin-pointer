CREATIVE DIRECTOR

GLOBETROTTER | DEVELOPER

Abby Mbuthi

A few months back I sent out an email to my travelling community sharing my amazing experiences, and tips for effectively locating cities around the globe for relocation or visit purposes. The response was incredible, as people wrote me back sharing their own encounter and challenges in the same. Accordingly, I complied the list of stories and identified a common recurring problem among many: Lack of information along with misinformation.
With that in mind, I thought it would be interesting to develop an application that comapres cost of living of capital cities in the world to share insights and knowledge with others who may be considering similar experiences in travelling.
